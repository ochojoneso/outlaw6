ABOUT OUTLAW 6

Live outlaw country and southern rock music cover band with a commitment to keeping the old music alive!
High energy band featuring 2 guitars acoustic and electric, bass guitar, a true pedal steel guitar, and drums.
We are currently booking shows in the NC/triangle area so contact us now to lock in a date.
